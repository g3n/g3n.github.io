Highlighted Project: Gokoban

Awarded 1st place in the 2017 Gopher Game Jam

Gokoban is a 3D puzzle game made with g3n. You control the Go gopher, and your objective in each level is to push the boxes until they are all on top of the yellow pads. There are elevators that help you reach high places and move boxes up and down.
